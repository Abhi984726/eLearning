## Securitization — Foundational Overview

**Topic:** Securitization process — concepts, stakeholders, 
and financial structures
**Duration:** 15 minutes
**Audience:** Finance professionals and related fields
**Tool:** Articulate Rise 360

### Learning Objectives
Upon completing this course, learners will be able to:
- Understand securitization and its process
- Identify key stakeholders involved
- Visualize the structure of a tranche
- Differentiate between credit ratings and 
  evaluate risk and creditworthiness

### Interactions Used
- Quiz
- Drag and drop
- Click to reveal
- Scenario based interactions
